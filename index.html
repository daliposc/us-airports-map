Major Airports of the United States

This leaflet map uses two geoJSON layers to show information about US airports. The data data were obtained through the instructor. I added two extra features beyond the basic requirements:

1. A search function for apriport abbreviations that zooms to the airport when searched.

2. The map markers resize based on zoom level to avoid overcrowding of markers when zoomed out.
